Max Temperature vs Latitude

Max temperature vs lattitude. Looking at the visualization we can see that as the cities are reaching a latitude close to zero the temperature is increasing. Interesting enough, there are some cities that are below the equator who have a higher temperature compared to their counter part cities. Some ways this data can be used is with retail stores. Looking at the visualization, we can see that cities that are closer to the equator will have a higher temperature. Retail stores make money by selling more clothes, so their market could grow beyond the skirts of the equator and more closer to the North and South Pole.
