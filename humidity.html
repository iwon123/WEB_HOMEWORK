Humidity vs Latitude

Humidity vs lattitude. Although humidity deals with heat, another important factor that humidity measures is the distance between the city and bodies of water. If you look at the world map and see where the equator aligns on the earth, the equator doesn't cover alot of land compared to the bodies of water. Based on the visualizations it looks that all the cities that have high humidity is not affected by how close that city is to the equator. Rather it is how close the city is to the water. There is no evidence of a strong correlation between the two factors.
